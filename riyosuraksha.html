RIYO SURAKSHA

To Make A Change In The Lifestyle On The NHAI And Make Indian Highways A Safer Place To Drive

This service is designed for commercial truck owners only. Indian truck market is dominated by individual truck owners who operate trucks in various sectors covering manufacturing, mining, agriculture, and almost every industry.


They play a significant role in transporting goods from one place to another through India. These truck owners' primary source of income is mainly from the trucking business. They spend most of the day unloading goods while ignoring their truck maintenance and inability to solve issues with drivers, RTO-related matters, and many more. If you are aware, it takes a lot of mental problems and causes financial pain.


Being lone truck owners, they have to run for almost everything to operate their trucks. Due to this tremendous pressure, there is a massive shortage of newcomers in this trucking business, affecting this trucking industry a lot.


Due to scattered infrastructure and lack of information, they feel economical, mental, and physical pressure and feel very helpless if any misfortune happens in the transit of goods. This is the main problem: no solution for them without reaching those places. The infrastructures are not very cooperative for single truck owners on the highway.


Although truck manufacturing companies have a vast network of service centers across India, their mobile service units are working very well. But the main problem is that all the manufacturing companies attend calls only for their own manufactured trucks. For old trucks, these facilities are costly, even for minor repair work.


Drivers arrange local mechanics, and for significant problems, truck owners reach the spot. This process adds additional cost and is time-consuming and, thus, a pain in the operation of trucks. Whether paper-related issues or repair or financial, or legal issues, it is a common issue in the transportation business on Indian National Highway.


We at RIO Rentitover are arranging all the necessary assistance for the truck owner to tackle this problem with the help of RIO IPOD on all Indian Highways with a single click on the RIO mobile application without going anywhere to their office or home.
